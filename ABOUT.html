ABOUT US

Chora General Secondary School is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education to students, based around the Southern part of Ethiopia. Our school is known for its quality of education, high-scoring students, and outstanding performance on different examinations such as the Ethiopian University Entrance Examination. We produce students who excel in both academics and extracurricular activities.
